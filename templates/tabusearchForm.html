? Beta is used to define the range of the distribution from which the students' budgets and the courses prices are drawn.

For example, if Beta = 4, the budgets and prices will be randomly selected within the range from 1 to beta+1 = 5.
`
? Delta is used to update the courses prices.
You can choose more than one delta and update the prices more than once.

For example, if delta is set to 0.5 and the price of course x is 5 so the update
price will be 5 + (5 * 0.5) = 7.5
